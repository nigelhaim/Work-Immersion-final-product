Naturalized ANGELO KAOUAME NOW NATURALIZED

06 | 02 | 2021

The Ivorian import center, Angelo Kouame, from Ateneo Blue Eagles finally acquired his long-awaited naturalization process. President Rodrigo Duterte signed the papers that made Kouame’s naturalization official.

His tweet that says “Pre, Filipino ako.” caused quite an uproar in social media as Gilas fans now expect his presence to boost the national team’s frontcourt.

Since height is a perennial struggle of the Philippine National Team, the inclusion of Angelo Kouame in the line-up will surely help the team’s pursuit of qualification in the Fiba Asia Cup Tournament this coming September.

According to Rappler, The 6-foot-10 Kouame averaged 12.5 points, 11.8 rebounds, 2.9 blocks, and 1.4 assists in UAAP Season 82, which was vital to Ateneo's 16-0 sweep and three-peat title romp.
